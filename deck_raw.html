Deck System

As you unlock specific Digimon, different Deck Buffs will be available to you.

Note: You can only have one Deck Buff active at one time!

For example, if I hatched all Four Holy Beasts, (Qinglongmon,Xuanwumon, Zhuqiaomon and Baihumon), I can have a certain effect as below::

  • Activation condition : Permanent t'ill Deactivated
  • Activation Effect : Critical hit damage 30%
  • Lasting time : Permanent
